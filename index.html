Being a Researcher
Being a researcher for the past six years has given me a new perspective to look at things. Before getting into research my methods were to directly indulge into the problem and look for the solution. In the past five years I have gained the sensibility to read and understand what others have done in the past and then find a solution to the problem. I am a lot more patient than what I was years ago. Research has also provided me an understanding to decide problems which are worth devoting time. It has also helped me to understand what interests me. As a whole it has helped me develop into a better being.
